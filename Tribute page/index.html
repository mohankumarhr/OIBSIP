Biography

Narayana Murthy, a renowned figure in the global technology industry, is widely recognized for his pivotal role in co-founding Infosys, one of India's most prominent IT services companies. Born on August 20, 1946, in Karnataka, India, Murthy's journey from humble beginnings to becoming a prominent business leader is nothing short of inspirational. In 1981, Narayana Murthy, along with six other co-founders, established Infosys with the vision of providing high-quality software services to clients worldwide. Under Murthy's leadership, Infosys pioneered the concept of offshore software development, leveraging India's pool of skilled technical talent to deliver cost-effective solutions to global clients. His foresight and strategic vision played a crucial role in positioning Infosys as a leading player in the global IT services industry. As the co-founder and former CEO of Infosys, Narayana Murthy played multiple roles within the organization, including setting strategic direction, fostering innovation, and promoting a culture of excellence and integrity. He emphasized the importance of ethical business practices, transparency, and accountability, which became integral to Infosys' corporate culture. Beyond his contributions to Infosys, Narayana Murthy is also known for his advocacy of corporate governance, social responsibility, and inclusive growth. He has been a vocal proponent of the role of businesses in driving positive societal change and sustainable development.
